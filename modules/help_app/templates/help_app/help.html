FAQs & Help

Welcome to the FAQs and Help Page! Here you’ll find guides, tips, and quick explanations to help you navigate and use the system smoothly.

1. Why can’t I draw immediately after joining a session?

You need to wait for the teacher to grant you drawing permissions. Once approved, the drawing tools will be enabled for you.

2. How do I join a session?

You can join either by scanning the session’s QR code or by entering the six-character Session Title.

3. Where can I view the sessions I’ve joined?

Click “My Dashboard” on the sidebar to view the list of all sessions you have joined.

4. How do I log out of my account?

Click your profile icon at the top-right corner and select “Log Out.”

5. What is the difference between Profile Management and Account Settings?

Profile Management allows you to view and customize how your profile appears to others. Meanwhile, Account Settings lets you update your account details, change your password, and adjust your account preferences.

6. Is my attendance immediately recorded once I join a session?

Yes. Your entry is logged automatically, and the teacher can see the time you joined and exited the session.

7. Why am I automatically logged out after changing my password?

For security purposes, the system logs you out to ensure your new password takes effect and to protect your account from unauthorized access.

8. Can I download my session content?

Yes. During a session, you can click the “Export” button located above the whiteboard to download the session.

9. Can I upload or insert photos during a session?

Yes. Use the “Upload” button to insert images onto the whiteboard.

10. As a teacher, how can I create a session?

Go to your dashboard and select “Manage Session.” Enter the required session details, then click “Create Session.” Inside the session details page, click “Invite” to generate the QR code or to copy the Session Title for students to join.
